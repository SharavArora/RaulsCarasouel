✦ Creative Rationale
Instagram Strategy

Emotion before information

Each slide uses one punchy line designed to stop the thumb mid-scroll. Self-deprecating opener mirrors Raul's real voice — grades, shock, disbelief. The "juggle" slide makes him relatable, not braggy.

LinkedIn Strategy

Credibility through vulnerability

Opens with proof, not résumé. Segmented slides (Founders vs Educators) make it immediately useful. Ends with a question — the highest-performing LI post ending. Insights are Raul's actual quotes from the summit.

Voice Consistency

Written as Raul, not about him

"My grades didn't get me here." / "I said Hi. That was it." / "This is my life now apparently." — every line references his own quote patterns. Short. Punchy. Never corporate. Never humble-braggy.

Platform Fit

Two different worlds, same story

IG: dark, cinematic, energetic — built for Gen Z scrollers. LinkedIn: editorial, clean, insight-forward — built for founders and educators making decisions. Same human, different masks.
